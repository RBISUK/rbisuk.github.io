Claim-Fix-AI — Turn tenant complaints into audit-ready cases

An intake funnel for repairs and disrepair that captures the right facts the first time, assembles evidence, and produces Ombudsman-ready packs. Fewer upheld complaints, calmer outcomes.

Outcomes

  • Clean chronology: events, dates, communications, media, readings.
  • Configurable SLA timers (e.g., 24/48/72h) with breach receipts.
  • Auto-generated case briefs and evidence bundles.
  • Lower uphold/exposure via standardised, fair handling.

Features

  • Structured intake: address, tenancy, issue taxonomy, severity triage.
  • Evidence capture: photos/video, damp/mould indicators, device logs.
  • Template letters & comms with regulator-safe defaults.
  • Exportable “audit pack”: timeline, contacts, actions, outcomes.

Psychology Loss aversion (stop uphold risk), clarity bias (pre-structured forms), authority (built on policy defaults), and friction design (only the next best action).

OmniAssist — AI colleague who never cuts corners

Your guarded automation partner. Executes repetitive online tasks with verifiable steps, screenshots, and logs. Does the boring bits without creating new risks.

Outcomes

  • Hours saved on case chasing, portal uploads, status checks.
  • Full reproducibility: “how we did it” log for each run.
  • Guardrails: domain allowlists, PII redaction, rate limits.

Features

  • Workflow builder with approvals & human-in-the-loop holds.
  • Step proofs: screenshots, selectors, API traces, timestamps.
  • Comms actions: polite nudges, deadline reminders, receipts.

Psychology Reduce cognitive load (one-click runs), trust via transparency (step logs), and commitment devices (pre-scheduled nudges).

RBIS Dashboard — Ops lit up, not buried in spreadsheets

One pane for repairs, complaints, and compliance. See breaches before they land, and wins as they happen.

Outcomes

  • SLA early-warning with “time-to-breach” badges.
  • Disrepair risk heatmaps: severity × duration × vulnerability.
  • Board-ready views and operational drilldowns.

Features

  • Metrics library: first-contact, fix time, uphold rate, comms cadence.
  • Export controls with auto-redaction options.
  • Role-based dashboards: Exec, Ops, Legal, Contractor.

Psychology Salience (breach countdowns), progress feedback (green paths), and social proof (team scorecards without naming/shaming).

NextusOne CRM — The CRM that speaks regulator + client

All the usual CRM power, plus compliance overlays that stop risky habits: lawful basis prompts, retention clocks, consent trails, and DSR tooling.

Overlays

  • Lawful-basis selector with purpose notes.
  • Retention schedules and auto-review tasks.
  • Consent capture, preference centres, PECR guards.

Safety by default

  • Role-based views and minimum-necessary data.
  • Evidence trails: who saw what, when, and why.
  • DSR workflows: access, rectification, erasure with receipts.

Psychology Default effect (safe presets), pre-commitment (retention timers), and trust badges (clear data handling).

Custom Sites & SEO-AI — Websites & SEO that survive audits

Fast, accessible sites with privacy-clean analytics and SEO that won’t melt under scrutiny. Speed, structure, and substance.

Delivery

  • Core Web Vitals tracked; WCAG 2.2 AA patterns baked-in.
  • Schema, sitemaps, robots, and crawl budget sanity.
  • Consent-respecting analytics; zero-dark-pattern UX.

Content engine

Psychology Clarity (plain-English CTAs), credibility (visible standards), and momentum (publish -> measure -> iterate loop).

PACT Ledger — Every promise made, kept, or called to account

Turn commitments into trackable objects with timers, nudges, and evidence. When things slip, the ledger shows exactly what happened.

Objects & flows

  • Promise objects with terms, parties, due dates, remedies.
  • Timers + nudges + escalation ladders.
  • Settlement playbooks and exportable packs.

Assurance

  • Immutable event trails and signed receipts.
  • Fairness prompts (proportionality, reasonableness).
  • Open-standard exports for dispute resolution.

Psychology Commitment & consistency (clear promises), reciprocity (mutual duties), and loss aversion (visible risk of breach).
