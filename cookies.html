Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files placed on your device. We aim to keep cookies minimal and respectful. You can control cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Categories

  • Strictly necessary — required for core site features (e.g., sessionless navigation polish).
  • Performance — simple page metrics to improve reliability (where enabled).
  • Functional — remember choices (e.g., dismissed banners).

We do not use invasive ad tech here.

Managing cookies

  • Browser controls: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari — search “clear cookies” / “block third-party cookies”.
  • Private browsing reduces persistence.

If you disable certain cookies, some features may not behave as expected.
