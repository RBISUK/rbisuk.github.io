Cookies & Tracking

We use only the minimum necessary cookies and storage needed to run the site and demos. Optional analytics is used only with your consent (where applicable).

What are cookies?

Small text files placed on your device to make websites work, keep you signed in, or measure usage. We also use localStorage for settings.

How we classify cookies

  • Strictly necessary (essential): required for security, navigation, and core features. These do not require consent.
  • Analytics (optional): helps us understand what’s working. Set only with consent if required by law.

Local storage

We may store simple flags like “dismissed banner”, “calculator inputs”, or “active tab” in your browser. You can clear these any time via your browser settings.

Managing cookies

  • Use your browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • If an in-site banner is shown, use it to accept/decline optional analytics.
  • Blocking essential cookies may break parts of the site.

Do Not Track

If your browser sends a Do-Not-Track signal, we treat that as an opt-out of optional analytics.

Changes

We’ll update this notice if our cookie use changes.
