Body Lounge — How we use it

Body Lounge is our lens for behaviour beneath words. We never “mind-read”. Instead, we:

  • Extract signal candidates (pauses, pitch drift, rhythm)
  • Tie to context (timeline, documents, comms provenance)
  • Document limitations and avoid bias (“no single cue” rule)
