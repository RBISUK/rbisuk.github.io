The biggest lie in business communication

It’s not “what you say vs how you say it.” It’s the behaviour behind both. Words can be rehearsed; tone can be performed. The interaction between language and delivery is where truth leaks out. RBIS studies that seam — lexical choices, cadence, hesitation, inconsistency — and turns it into defensible insight.

  • Corporate negotiations — surface hesitation before it becomes obstruction.
  • Risk & compliance — find inconsistencies and undisclosed vulnerabilities.
  • Leadership & hiring — assess confidence, congruence, and control.

Beyond words

Communication is only partly verbal. Acoustic features and timing often reveal state: stress, inconsistency, or confidence. RBIS transforms those signals into actionable intelligence.

Disciplinary tribunals and regulators punish concealment more than error. Behavioural cues and timeline integrity can make the difference between escalation and resolution.
