Privacy Policy

Plain-English policy. For controller/processor specifics, see the Data Processing Addendum.

What we collect

  • Operational signals: intake fields, event timestamps, actor IDs, export artefacts.
  • Support data: communications you send us (emails, forms).
  • Website telemetry: strictly necessary cookies; optional analytics/marketing only with opt-in.

Why we collect it

  • Provide the service (contract) — run timers, build evidence packs, exports.
  • Improve reliability (legitimate interests) — detect abuse, performance tuning.
  • Marketing (consent) — only when you opt-in.

How long we keep it

Evidence artefacts follow your retention schedule or legal obligations; ephemeral logs rotate. We honour deletion requests when lawful.

Your rights

  • Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time.

International transfers

Transfers outside UK/EEA use appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs). Details available on request.
