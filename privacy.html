Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and handle personal data fairly, lawfully, and transparently. This notice explains what we collect, why, and how you can exercise your rights.

Who we are

RBIS (Roberts Behavioural & Intelligence Services) (“we”, “us”). For privacy queries, use Contact or email Contact@RBISIntelligence.com.

  • Controller: RBIS
  • Site hosting: GitHub Pages

Data we collect

  • Contact data (name, email, phone, organisation) when you submit forms.
  • Case/demo text you paste into our demo or tools (you are responsible for redaction).
  • Operational logs (timestamps, page views, simple analytics). We aim to keep this minimal.
  • Cookies — see Cookie Policy.

Why we use your data (lawful bases)

  • Provide services & respond (contract/legitimate interests).
  • Improve and secure our site (legitimate interests).
  • Legal/Compliance — record keeping, responding to lawful requests (legal obligation).
  • Marketing — only with consent where required (you can withdraw anytime).

Sharing

We share data only when necessary:

  • Hosting/infra (e.g., GitHub Pages) to serve this site.
  • Form handling (Formspree) when you submit Contact.
  • Advisers/Legal if reasonably required to protect our rights or comply with law.

We do not sell personal data.

International transfers

Some processors may store data outside the UK/EEA. Where they do, we aim to rely on adequacy regulations or standard contractual clauses.

Retention

  • Contact enquiries: typically 12–24 months.
  • Operational logs: short, rolling windows where possible.
  • Contracts/invoices: 6+ years (legal/accounting).

We delete or anonymise when no longer needed.

Your rights

  • Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection (including to direct marketing).
  • Withdraw consent where used.
  • Complain to the ICO (UK) — but please contact us first so we can help.

Security

  • HTTPS by default; principle of least privilege; audit-friendly logging.
  • We avoid unnecessary data collection; you should avoid pasting sensitive data into demos.

Children

Our services are for professional/organisational use. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this notice. Material changes will be sign-posted here with a new “Updated” date.
