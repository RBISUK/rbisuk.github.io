Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Who we are

Controller: Ryan Roberts Behavioural & Intelligence Services (“RBIS”, “we”, “us”). We provide behavioural and intelligence analysis, reports and supporting software.

Contact: privacy@rbis.uk (preferred) or write to RBIS, UK. We will respond to privacy requests within 30 days.

What we collect

  • Identity & contact: name, email, phone, organisation, role.
  • Case materials: documents, emails, transcripts, recordings, images, timelines, metadata and notes you supply for analysis.
  • Usage & device: pages viewed, timestamps, approximate region, non-identifying diagnostics. We run minimal analytics only.
  • Commercial: quotes, orders, invoices, payments (processed via your chosen provider; we do not store card numbers).
  • Recruitment: CVs, cover letters, portfolio links and interview notes.

Lawful bases (UK GDPR)

  • Contract: to provide reports, software, and managed services you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure systems, prevent abuse, improve service, and keep necessary business records.
  • Consent: for optional communications and non-essential cookies.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with tax, accounting, or court orders.

How we use data

  • Delivering analysis and producing court-ready/board-ready reports.
  • Operating the website, demos, dashboards and calculators.
  • Customer support, incident investigation, and quality assurance.
  • Creating de-identified statistics to improve workflows and templates.

Sharing

We share only when necessary:

  • Processors: hosting, email, storage, project tools—under written contracts and only on our instructions.
  • Professional advisers: accountants, insurers, legal counsel.
  • Legal: where required by law or to defend legal claims.

We do not sell personal data.

International transfers

Where data moves outside the UK/EEA, we use recognised safeguards (UK/EEA standard contractual clauses, adequacy, or equivalent contractual protections).

Retention

  • Client case files: typically 12–24 months after closure unless you request earlier deletion or we must retain longer for legal reasons.
  • Business records: invoices/contracts for up to 7 years (legal obligation).
  • Recruitment: 12 months for unsuccessful candidates (or sooner on request).
  • Backups: cyclic, time-limited and auto-purged.

Security

  • Encryption in transit; restricted access; principle of least privilege.
  • Audit logging for sensitive operations.
  • Data minimisation; we ask only for what we need.

Your rights

You can: access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and obtain a portable copy. You may withdraw consent at any time (it won’t affect past lawful use).

Contact: privacy@rbis.uk. You can also complain to the UK ICO.

Children

Our services are business-focused and not designed for children.

Third-party links

External links are operated under their own privacy terms.

Changes

If we materially change this notice, we’ll update the date and, where appropriate, notify you.
