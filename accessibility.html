Accessibility Statement (WCAG 2.2 AA)

RBIS is committed to accessibility for everyone. We aim to meet WCAG 2.2 AA across our site and exports.

Measures we take

  • Color contrast ≥ 4.5:1; focus styles and visible outlines
  • Keyboard-only navigation and logical heading order
  • Reduced-motion media query respected; no autoplaying media
  • Labels for interactive controls; ARIA only when semantic roles aren’t enough
  • Captions/transcripts for media; BSL options in OmniAssist

Scope

This statement covers the brochure site (rbisintelligence.com) and typical PDF/CSV exports we generate as evidence packs. Operational apps have their own accessibility roadmaps and audits.

Known limitations & fixes

  • Historic blog posts or third-party embeds may have minor issues; we will remediate on request.
  • Legacy PDFs are being replaced with accessible templates.

Feedback & contact

If you encounter barriers, email accessibility@rbisintelligence.com with the page URL and a short description. We will respond promptly and propose a fix or workaround.

Enforcement

If you are dissatisfied with our response, UK users can contact the Equality Advisory and Support Service (EASS).
