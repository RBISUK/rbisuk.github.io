FAQ

Is this always-on voice?

No. Voice is opt-in. Default is off; calls are handled via telephony connectors with explicit consent notices.

Can we block certain callers?

Yes — do-not-answer lists and rate limits are supported.

Where is data stored?

Region-pinned UK/EU storage with retention policies per plan.
