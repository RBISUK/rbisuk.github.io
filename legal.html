Accessibility Statement

We aim for a usable, accessible experience for everyone. Our UI uses strong color contrast, keyboard-visible focus rings, reduced-motion support, and large touch targets.

  • Standards: WCAG 2.2 AA (best efforts, ongoing).
  • Keyboard: All interactive elements are reachable with Tab / Shift+Tab; current item is clearly highlighted.
  • Reduced motion: Respects prefers-reduced-motion; ornamental animation is disabled where set.
  • Touch targets: Minimum 44px height; safe-area insets supported on iOS/Android.

If you experience any barrier, tell us via the Contact page (select “Accessibility”). We’ll review and fix.
