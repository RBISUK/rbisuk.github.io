Privacy Policy

We collect only the signals needed to deliver the service: intake details, event timestamps, actors, and export artefacts. We never sell personal data.

Lawful bases: contract, legitimate interests, and consent where applicable (e.g., marketing).

Retention: evidence artefacts are retained per client policy and legal obligations; ephemeral logs rotate.

Questions? Make a DSAR or contact us via the Demo intake.

Terms of Service

RBIS provides evidence-intelligence software and services. We operate clocks, assemble packs, and export artefacts; we do not provide legal advice.

  • Uptime and support per your plan (see Managed Support).
  • Export formats: CSV/PDF + exhibit pointers.
  • You own your data; we hold it as a processor where applicable.
For enterprise terms, see the Data Processing Addendum.

Cookies & Consent

We minimise cookies. Where marketing or analytics cookies are present, we use prior consent and clear categories.

  • Strictly necessary: auth/session (if signed-in features exist).
  • Analytics/marketing: only with opt-in.
Test consent flows in the Demo.

Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

When acting as a processor, we follow controller instructions, maintain appropriate security, and support subject rights.

  • Subprocessors disclosed on request.
  • Deletion on termination or handover of export packs.
Need a signed DPA? Contact us via Managed Support.

Service Disclosure

We are evidence specialists. We analyse the records and events in front of us and assemble them into audit-ready outputs. We are not a law firm.
