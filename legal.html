Legal & Policies

Independent Analysis — Not Legal Advice

RBIS UK does not provide legal advice or representation. We act as independent analysts and produce court-ready, evidence-based reports designed to be submitted alongside a solicitor’s work. Our commitment is clarity, integrity, and no bias.

Data & Privacy

  • We minimise data collected and retain only what is necessary.
  • We practice least-privilege access and log handling steps for audit.
  • On request we can provide DPIA/ROPA entries and vendor due-diligence notes.

For procurement packs (insurance, security, accessibility statements), email: Contact@RBISIntelligence.com

Acceptable Use & Conduct

  • No attempts to mislead or conceal material facts.
  • No unlawful acquisition of data or breach of confidentiality.
  • We may refuse work where independence or safety is compromised.
